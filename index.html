RiceCAST is an online tool forecasting the yield of rice in different regions of the Philippines which bases on the historical data of yield, historical rainfall and normal rainfall values, the climate type and rice ecosystems existing within a selected region.

Find Out More

More about RiceCAST

It applies a machine learning approach, called Random Forests, to achieve accurate predictions. It aims to prepare the governing agencies against impending economic problems and improve decision making skills and strategies used in farming to strengthen the food security the nation needs.
